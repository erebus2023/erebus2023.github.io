Registration

There are three forms we need you to fill out: registration, meals/lodging reservation, and a logistics Google form.

You can register for the conference here. The cost of registration is $250. This form will ask you for other details like dietary restrictions and additional accommodations.

We are still working on the meals/lodging request form. Here you will be able to specify how many nights you will stay and meals you will participate in. Please note that Biosphere 2 is very isolated and there are little to no food options nearby, so you should sign up for meals. Also, if you are bringing a guest that you will share a room with, please contact the organizers separately at erebusmeeting2023@gmail.com for a separate payment procedure.

Finally, we ask that you fill out this Google form for logistics, including flight info, transportation to/from Biosphere 2, the Wednesday afternoon excursion, casita housemates, and other fun things.

If you are local to the University of Arizona, you only need to fill out the Google form. We will contact you separately for account numbers, dietary restrictions, and nights you are staying.

We look forward to seeing the EREBUS folks in October!
